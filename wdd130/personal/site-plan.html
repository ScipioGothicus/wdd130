Content

Home page

This site will be designed to demonstrate talents, skills, and projects I have as a person and to compile it into a portfolio that is easily presentable to potential employers or investigators. The people I intend to appeal to are companies or individuals hiring employees with my certain skillset, but I also want to welcome anyone with an interest in programming or graphic design.

Images for the Home page

Q&A

Hopefully, questions about how long I have been practicing programming and/or graphic design will be answered by visiting my site. I will add timelines (probably in a biography section) showing how long I have worked on these skills. Also, of course, the question of if I am currently unemployed or free to do commissions for a person in question will hopefully be answered from my site!

About

This information about me will be the most concise on my site, of course! Sites like LinkedIn or Indeed lack the same creative freedom that my site would allow for. I would be able to display my achievements and portfolio-worthy content in a much more presentable manner than if I put this information on a job site like the previously mentioned LinkedIn/Indeed.
